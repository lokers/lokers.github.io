Why, How, Where

I constantly look to improve the surrounding world and make things better. I think it's our human duty to create and improve, and when I see oportunity, I grab it. I like to challenge myself, raise the bar and learn new things. Once done, I move on to the next big thing.

Working with the best people I can find around me, I watch them and take the most out of it. I also learn from my own mistakes, accept them and move on. Some people laugh that I think even when I sleep and I dream solutions during the night. The truth is, I won't sleep well until I found one.

I got my education in Lodz, Poland. For the past ten years I've been working and living in London and it seems like this is the best place for me to meet people that create innovative solutions. You can catch me in both cities. I constantly fly between these two beautiful but so different worlds, taking the best out of both.
